23-Sep-2022 (last update 17-Oct-2022)

As the title says I’m going to be present at Nerdearla.

This will be my second time that I attend to this event since I’ve been there for the first time in 2019. I remember that John ‘Maddog’ Hall was making a presentation called “50 years of UNIX and the landing on the Moon”, you can find a record of the presentation here.

It was my first talk ever and I loved it, sadly becouse of the pandemic I couldn’t go in the past two years.

The event is half remote half presential and the entrance is free. It takes place at Centro cultural Konex on Once, Buenos Aires on Oct 19 to Oct 22, last three days.
